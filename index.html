FAQ

1. What are restaurant's opening hours?

Opening hours are Monday ~ Saturday, from 11:00 to 21:00.

2. Do I need to make a reservation in advance?

We recommend you make a reservation, especially during peak hours, weekends, and holidays.

3. Do you offer vegetarian or gluten-free options?

Our menu includes vegetarian and gluten-free options. Please check our menu or ask the server for specific choices.

4. Can I bring my own drinks?

Bringing outside drinks is not allowed, but we have a wide selection of drinks.

5. Does the restaurant offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services. You can order through our website or via our delivery platform partners.

6. Does the restaurant offer delivery services?

7. Can I bring my pets to the restaurant?

No, pets are not allowed to enter to the restaurant.

8. Does the restaurant have a kids' play area?

Yes, we have a play area designed for children, so the whole family can enjoy a pleasant dining experience.

9. Does the restaurant have parking spaces?

No, we do not have parking spaces. Please take public transportation to the restaurant.

10. Can I takeout my meals?

Yes, we offer takeout services. You can order food in the restaurant and take it with you.

11. What should I do if I have a food allergy?

If you have a food allergy, please inform our server, and we will provide suitable menu options based on your needs. Please see the contact information of the website if you have any need.
