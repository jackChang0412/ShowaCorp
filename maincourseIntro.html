Main Courses

Pure ingredients and refined techniques.
From simmered dishes to grilled specialties, each dish showcases authentic flavors and craftsmanship.

照り焼きチキン (Teriyaki Chicken)

Juicy chicken thigh glazed with a sweet and savory teriyaki sauce
and served with white rice for a classic Japanese taste.

Pairs well with: White rice, onsen egg, miso soup

鯖の味噌煮 (Miso-Simmered Mackerel)

Tender mackerel simmered in rich miso sauce, blending deep umami with natural fish flavors.

Pairs well with: White rice, Japanese pickles, chawanmushi

天丼 (Tempura Rice Bowl)

Crispy tempura shrimp and vegetables served over rice, drizzled with a sweet-savory sauce.

Pairs well with: Miso soup, pickled daikon, Japanese tea

カツカレー (Japanese Curry with Pork Cutlet)

Crispy breaded pork cutlet topped with thick, aromatic Japanese curry, a beloved comfort food.

Pairs well with: Onsen egg, shredded cabbage, pickled red ginger

すき焼き (Sukiyaki – Japanese Beef Hotpot)

Thinly sliced beef, tofu, and vegetables simmered in a sweet-savory soy-based sauce for a rich, comforting taste.

Pairs well with: Raw egg yolk, udon noodles, Japanese sake
