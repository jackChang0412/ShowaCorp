Dessert

Showa-era desserts, like silky purin and refreshing mitsu-mame, evoke nostalgia with their simple, comforting sweetness and timeless charm.

プリン

A silky, caramel-flavored custard pudding, inspired by Western flan but with a uniquely Japanese texture and sweetness.

みつ豆

A refreshing dessert made of agar jelly cubes, sweet red beans, and seasonal fruits, often drizzled with black sugar syrup.

どら焼き

A beloved treat featuring two fluffy castella-like pancakes filled with sweet red bean paste, enjoyed by both children and adults alike
