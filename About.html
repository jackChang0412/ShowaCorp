About Us

Our Story

Founded in 1950, Showa Catering Group is a restaurant dedicated to serving authentic Japanese cuisine. Inspired by the traditional Japanese concept of 旬の味 (in season), we emphasize the use of the freshest local ingredients to present authentic Japanese flavors in the purest way possible.

Each cuisine is a tribute to Japanese culinary culture. We believe that food is not only a feast for the palate, but also a cultural heritage. That's why our meals not only incorporate traditional techniques, but also modern creativity, in the hope of providing our customers with the ultimate dining experience.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with authentic hospitality and passionate food while fostering remarkable experiences in their daily life.

Our Vision

To become Asia’s most heartwarming Japanese dining brand, making "Showa" a cherished part of everyday life.

Core Values

Craftsmanship & Precision: We uphold traditional handcraft and attention to detail, sharing the beauty of authentic Japanese cuisine.

Omotenashi (Hospitality)

With a customer-first mindset, we strive to create warm and welcoming dining experiences.

Innovative Fusion

Blending tradition with creativity, we craft a unique culinary culture that evolves with the times.

Sustainability

We value the origin of our ingredients and actively promote eco-friendly dining and carbon reduction initiatives.

Our Feactures

  • Fresh seafood delivery: The freshest seafood is purchased daily from the Tsukiji market and local ports.
  • Authentic Japanese Cuisine: Experienced Japanese chefs lead the kitchen, guaranteeing the quality of every dish..
  • Japanese Ambiance: The interior features a minimalist Japanese design, creating a serene and cozy dining experience.
  • Healthy and Delicious: We use minimal oil and salt to highlight the ingredients' natural flavors.
  • Curated Sake Pairing: We offer a diverse selection of sake and plum wine imported from Japan to elevate your dining experience.
